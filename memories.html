First time having a job

During the summer of 2023, I worked at 7-Eleven to step out of my comfort zone and experience a change of pace.

A month sounds really short, yet it is so delightful. During that time, I worked for the first time and received my first salary. You can say that it was my first for everything. I rarely did household chores, let alone cooking. Despite being an introvert, who hesitates in communication and often stays in comfort zone, that did not stop me from going out there to improve myself.

If Hung can balance work and study in the future, perhaps he will continue with part-time jobs.
